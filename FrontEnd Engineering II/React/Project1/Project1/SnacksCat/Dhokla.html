Prep: 5 minuntes || Cook: 15 minutes || Serves: 4

This fluffy and soft dhokla can be made just in 20 minutes!!

Ingredients:

Method:





















Step 1

Take all ingredients to prepare batter. Pour approx. 2-3 cups water in steamer at bottom and heat it over medium flame. Make sure that steamer is at least 4-5 minutes heated before placing the plates in it. Grease 2 small plates or thalis using 1 tsp oil.

Step 2

Take 1 cup gram flour, 1 ttbsp semolina, 1½ tsp lemon juice, 1 tsp green chilli-ginger paste, 1/4 cup curd, 3/4 cup water and salt in a bowl. Mix them properly into smooth batter. Make sure that there are no lumps.

Step 3

Add 1 tsp fruit salt in batter and stir in one direction for approx. 1 minute. You will notice its size would increase to almost double. Now, pour batter immediately into each greased plate and fill it upto 1/2-inch thickness.

Step 4

Place plates in steamer and steam for 10-12 minutes over medium flame. After 10-12 minutes, insert a knife or toothpick into dhokla and check if it comes out clean. Takeout plates from steamer and let them cool for few minutes. Cut fluffy khaman dhokla into small squares using knife.

Step 5

Heat 2 tbsp oil in a small pan or tempering pan. Add 1/2 tsp mustard seeds and a pinch of hing. When seeds begin to crackle, add 1/2 tsp cumin seeds, 1 tsp sesame seeds, 10-15 curry leaves and 4 green chillies. Sauté them for few seconds.

Step 6

Add 1/3 cup water and 1 tbsp sugar and bring it to a boil; let it cook for a minute over high flame. Tempering(tadka) is ready, pour it over dhoklas and toss gently until each dhokla is coated well with tempering.

Step 7

Garnish with 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves. Serve with green coriander chutney.
