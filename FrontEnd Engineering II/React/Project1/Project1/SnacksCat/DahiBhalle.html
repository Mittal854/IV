Prep: 10 minuntes || Cook: 30 minutes || Serves: 3

This tangy and sweet Dahi Bhalla recipe will satisfy all your cravings.

Ingredients:

Method:
















Step 1

First of all, soak 1 cup urad dal for 5 hours. Drain the water and transfer to the mixer. Also, add 1 chilli and 1 inch ginger.

Step 2

Add water as required and blend into a smooth paste. Transfer the urad dal batter to a large bowl.

Step 3

Take 1/4 cup soaked (2 hours) moong dal in blender. Blend into smooth paste and transfer to the bowl of urad dal batter. Now add 1 tsp salt and mix well.

Step 4

Now using a wet spoon or hands, drop a spoonful of batter into the hot oil. Keep the flame on medium, stir occasionally. Fry until the vada turns golden brown and crisp. Drain the vada on kitchen paper to remove excess oil.

Step 5

Now take 5 cups of hot water in a large bowl. Add salt and hing. Mix well and make sure it is well combined. Now put the hot fried vada in water and submerge it completely. Soak for 30 minutes or until the vada absorbs the water. After 30 minutes, squeeze out the water and transfer to a plate.

Step 6

First of all, take 2 cups curd, 2 tbsp sugar and 1/2 tsp salt. Whisk and mix well making sure everything is well combined.

Step 7

First of all pour sweet curd on the vada. Also add green chutney and a generous amount of tamarind chutney. Sprinkle chilli powder, cumin powder, chaat masala. Top with boondi and coriander leaves.

Step 8

Finally, enjoy chilled dahi bhalla.
