Prep: 35 minuntes || Cook: 10 minutes || Serves: 20

Gulab Jamun is the type of dessert which can't put down no matter what!

Ingredients:

Method:












Step 1

Take khoya in a bowl. Mash it very well. There should be no lumps. Then add grated paneer, sooji, maida, baking powder and cardamom powder to the mashed khoya.

Step 2

Press the high pressure or manual button and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes if using an Instant Pot. Let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes and then do a quick pressure release.

Step 3

Mix well. Add milk and gather together to form a dough with milk. Don't knead. Just gently mix. Cover the dough and keep aside for 30 mins. Make small balls from the dough. Cover the balls and keep aside.

Step 4

Dissolve sugar in water. Heat the sugar solution till it become sticky. Add rose water and stir. Keep the sugar solution aside. On cooling if the sugar syrup crystallizes, then just add 2 to 3 tbsp water and warm the syrup again. It will again return to a liquid state.

Step 5

Meanwhile, while the sugar syrup is cooking, heat oil till its medium hot. Lower the flame and wait for a minute. Then gently place the dough balls in the oil. Once they start to have tiny golden spots, keep on rotating them in the oil, so that the jamun are evenly browned. Remove the fried jamun and then drain them on kitchen paper towels to remove excess oil.

Step 6

Then place the hot fried dough balls in the sugar syrup. Continue frying the rest of the dough balls in batches. When all the jamuns are placed in the sugar syrup, then keep the whole pan with the sugar syrup and the gulab jamun, on a low heat for 1 to 2 minutes till the jamun become soft.

Step 7

Heating helps the gulab jamun to absorb the syrup and become soft. The jamun increase a bit in size. Don't overcook as then the jamun can break. Use a large pan, so that the jamuns are not overcrowded and you can easily stir them gently while they are simmering.

Step 8

Serve gulab jamun warm or at room temperature. Garnish with rose petals or almond slivers.
