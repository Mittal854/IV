Prep: 20 minuntes || Cook: 40 minutes || Serves: 6

This vegetarian dish is full of flavor and makes an easy weeknight meal.

Ingredients:

Method:
















Step 1

Heat oil in a pan on medium heat. Once hot, add 2 small roughly chopped yellow onions and cook for 2 to 3 minutes until it turns soft and very light brown in color. Then add ginger garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.

Step 2

Add tomatoes, green chlli and salt. Stir and cook the tomatoes for 5 minutes until they are very soft. Remove the pan from heat and let it cool down. Then transfer to a blender and grind everything to a smooth paste and set aside.

Step 3

Heat ghee and remaining oil in a pan on medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Then add Kashmiri red chili powder. Stir and immediately add the prepared masala paste. Mix until masala is well combined with the chili powder.

Step 4

Then add the spices. Mix until combined, then add tomato paste and salt. Stir and cook the masala for 6 to 7 minutes.

Step 5

At this point add paneer and green peas and mix well. Then add some water and salt. Stir everything and cover and let cook for 3-4 minutes.

Step 6

Serve matar paneer hot with rotis or paratha.
